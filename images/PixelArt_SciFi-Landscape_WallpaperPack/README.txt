Free Pixel Art Wallpaper Pack

A set of 5 different free sci-fi themed pixel art landscape pieces in (roughly) wallpaper-suitable resolutions. Each artwork comes in three different resolutions.

Pieces included:

"Above ruins"
"Behind the mountains"
"By the lake"
"Distant purple"
"Disturbed tranquility"
